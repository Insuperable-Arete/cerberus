What am I looking at?

Cerfer.us provides 24 hours of the most current surf forecast data from Magic Seaweed, repackaged and animated to illustrate a map level overview.

How do I read this map?

Each colored circle on the map represents a beach in the MSW database.

The color of the beach marker represents the swell quality/power.

weakstrong

The length, speed and orientation of the yellow vectors represent wind speed and direction. (Below is a 5mph NW wind)

What can I infer from this map?

Generally speaking, if the circle is dark and wind is light and blowing off-shore, you should probably go paddle out around that vicinity.
